Kris Aquino is a famous Filipino TV Host. The multi-talented Host is also a actress, producer, product endorser, and businesswoman. She has been actively working in the industry for almost four decades. She has been part of various talk shows and game shows.

Kris Aquino was born as Kristina Bernadette Cojuangco Aquino on February 14, 1971, in Quezon City, Philippines. Kris is among the five children born to Benigno S. Aquino, Jr. and his wife, Corazon Cojuangco-Aquino. Her father was a senator, and her mother served as the 11th President of the Philippines. She graduated from the Ateneo de Manila University in 1992 with a Bachelor of Arts in English Literature.

Relationship Status of Kris Aquino

Kris Aquino is not only famous for her successful career but also famous for her relationships. Kris's relationship history dates back as early as her career started. Kris seems to be single as of 2020. She is enjoying her life spending time with her siblings and loved ones. 

Kris was in a formal relationship with Philip Salvador in 1995. Kris and Philip were having an extra-marital affair at the time. From this relationship, Kris gave birth to their son, Joshua, in 1995. This relationship never remained the same after the birth of Joshua. 

The Filipino actress remained single for some time and then found love with Joey Marquez. This happy looking relationship also ended in 2003 after Aquino exposed Marquez on contracting her with STD. 

Failed Marriage with James Yap

After two heartbreaking incident's Aquino married James Yap. James is a former professional basketball player. The love birds dated for some time, eventually tying their knot on July 10, 2005. They had a civil marriage ceremony. 

In 2006, Aquino was pregnant with their first child. There were many rumors of Yap's infidelity at the same time. In April 2007, Aquino gave birth to her second son, James Carlos "Bimby" Aquino Yap, Jr., in Makati City.

To Everyone's surprise, In 2010, Aquino announced that she had separated from Yap. Also, She was seeking an annulment. Initially, the reason for their separation was not shared with the public. After two years, In February 2012, Their marriage was declared null and void. She was then in a relationship with Herbert Bautista for a short time in 2014.

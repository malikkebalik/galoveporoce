BTS's V (Kim Taehyung) Hits Milestone of 800 Million Spotify Streams with Just 3 OSTs!!

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  448 words  |  Martina Birk

Kim Taehyung, popularly known as V of BTS, continues to make waves and achieve remarkable records even before his highly anticipated solo debut. Recently, Taehyung surpassed an incredible milestone of 800 million accumulated streams on Spotify, solidifying his position as a global streaming powerhouse:

#V has surpassed 800 million streams on Spotify (all credits)! pic.twitter.com/OrUK1tSkAS

— BTS Charts & Votings (@btschartstudio) July 13, 2023

Notably, this achievement is particularly remarkable as he has yet to release a solo album. Taehyung attained this feat through his exceptional contributions to three K-drama OSTs, namely “Christmas Tree,” “Sweet Night,” and “It’s Definitely You.”

Establishing a Reputation as the “OST King”

Taehyung’s remarkable success on Spotify further reinforces his reputation as the “OST King.” His OST tracks have resonated deeply with listeners, leading to their immense popularity and enduring presence on the platform.

Notably, he currently holds the distinction of having the top two most-streamed Korean OSTs on Spotify. The unwavering support and love from fans worldwide have contributed to this outstanding accomplishment:

Building Anticipation for his Debut Album

Taehyung’s remarkable performance and the enduring appeal of his OSTs have only heightened excitement for his upcoming debut album, rumored to be released later this year.

Fans eagerly anticipate his solo endeavors and eagerly await the moment when Taehyung showcases his artistry in a full-fledged solo release. The anticipation surrounding his debut album is a testament to the immense talent and creativity he brings to the table.

Who is Jairek Robbins?

Jairek Robbins is an American performance coach and motivational speaker and the son of the famous American life coach, motivational speaker, and author, Tony Robbins.

In 2014, Jarek wrote the book, ‘Live It!: Achieve Success By Living With Purpose’.

How old is Jairek Robbins?

Jairek Robbins was born as Jairek Anthony Robbins on May 26, 1984, in Santa Monica, California, USA. As of 2023, his age is 38. He was born to parents Tony Robbins (father) and Liz Acosta (mother).

His father, Tony Robbins is an American public speaker, author, life coach, and philanthropist best known for his seminars, infomercials, and self-help books including Unlimited Power, and Awaken the Giant Within.

His parents never got married and later broke up. He has a half-sister named Jolie Jenkins who is an actress famous for her portrayal of Agent Leyla Harrison in ‘The X-Files’.

His ethnicity is English.

Education: School/College, University

Talking about his educational background, Jairek Robbins went to the University of San Diego. From there, he graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in psychology.

Jairek Robbins: Professional Life, Career

While still studying, he took a semester abroad through the Semester at Sea program and visited Japan, China, India, Thailand,  Vietnam, Tanzania, Brazil, South Africa, and Venezuela.

He revealed in an interview with Forbes that this was the inspiration for much of his work and focus on finding a way to make a positive difference in the world through volunteerism and philanthropy.

He started his career in 2002 with the Anthony Robbins Foundation where he worked for two years. While in college, he spent months living in a rural farming village and volunteered to teach organic farming in Uganda.

Moreover, Jairek Robbins was a board member for “Just Like My Child”, a non-profit organization that also does health and education projects in Uganda.

Robbins also hosts a weekly YouTube segment entitled JRCtv and is a regular contributor to the Huffington Post blog.

In 2013, he delivered a TEDx talk in New York City titled, ‘A Simple Formula To Inspire the World To Live Their Dreams’.

Jairek Robbins later became a Results Coach at Robbins Research International. From 2006 to 2008, he served as a representative of the Results Coach at Robbins International.

In 2008, he received a Congressional Award Gold Medal. It is a non-competitive award for young American citizens who have engaged in activities such as voluntary public service

In 2010, he founded Jairek Robbins Companies and just a year later, he started Rapid Results Retreat.

In 2014, Jairek Robbins became a New York Times Best Seller with a book titled, ‘Live It!: Achieve Success By Living With Purpose’.

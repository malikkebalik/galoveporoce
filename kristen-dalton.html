Career

Dalton got her first break in 1989 when she appeared in Tango & Cash alongside Kurt Russell. In 1998, she had a small part in A Night at the Roxbury, a comedy starring Will Ferrell and Chris Kattan. In The Departed, she played Frank Costello’s girlfriend Gwen, played by Jack Nicholson (2006).

Dalton is best known for her role as reporter Dana Bright in Anthony Michael Hall’s USA Network series The Dead Zone. Beverly Hills, 90210, Murder, She Wrote, Diagnosis Murder, Sliders, Stargate SG-1, and CSI: NY are among Dalton’s other television appearances. Danielle Steel’s Family Album, They Nest, Surviving Gilligan’s Island (2001) (in which she played actress Tina Louise), Babysitter Wanted, and Gleason are among her TV credits.

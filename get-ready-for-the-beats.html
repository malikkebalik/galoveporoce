At midnight Eastern Time (UTC) tonight, Nicki Minaj will release Pink Friday 2, her highly anticipated second studio album. Fans can listen to it on Spotify and Apple Music. Fans are curious about the album’s tracklist because the rap icon has been mum about details surrounding it.

Which Songs Are Included on Nicki Minaj’s Pink Friday 2?

Nearly an hour before the album’s release, Minaj announced the tracks on Instagram. Among the many artists featured on the album are Future, Drake, J. Cole, Lil Uzi Vert, and Lil Wayne. The album’s intro, “Are You Gone Already?” was co-produced by Billie Eilish and Finneas, as Minaj revealed on Twitter.

After postponing the release of Pink Friday 2 in October, Minaj announced that her new album would be available for download on December 8, the day she turned 41. The new album for this amazing collection of songs, of which Minaj is very proud, will be released on my birthday, which is a special day for both her and the Barbz. “I am overjoyed to share the news that Pink Friday 2 will be released on my birthday.”

Minaj postponed the album’s release from October 20 to November 17 after initially scheduling it for October 20. Minaj claimed on Twitter that “some really exciting news that I’ll share with you guys @ a later time” was the reason behind the second delay, though she didn’t specify when it happened.

After the groundbreaking 2010 debut album Pink Friday and 2018’s Queen, Minaj will release Pink Friday 2. In 2021, Minaj reissued her mixtape Beam Me Up Scotty, which she released in 2009, and she hinted at a tour that would begin in early 2024.

While speaking with Vogue recently, the artist reflected on her past work and how she approached Pink Friday 2. “I wonder where I was in a lot of my songs when I listen to them now,” she confessed. “I reverted to my previous strategy for this album.”

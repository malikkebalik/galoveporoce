At its core, “Like a Cowboy” reflects the struggle of moving on from a past love. The lyrics delve into the internal turmoil and conflicting emotions that arise when trying to let go of someone who has left a lasting impact. The narrator is torn between longing for the connection they once had and realizing the need to move forward with their own life.

Throughout the song, McCollum eloquently paints a picture of a cowboy, drawing parallels between the character’s solitary journey and his own emotional journey. This imagery adds depth to the sentiment, illustrating the strength and resilience required to heal from heartbreak. The lyrics evoke a sense of vulnerability and raw honesty, giving listeners a glimpse into the artist’s personal experiences and emotions.

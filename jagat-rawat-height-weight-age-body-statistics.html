Jagat Rawat is an Indian film and television actor and theatre artist who has appeared in movies like Commando – A One Man Army, Manjhi – The Mountain Man, No One Killed Jessica, Yeh Faasley, Happy Bhag Jayegi, Hijack, Raaz 3D, Aurangzeb, Chor Chor Super Chor, Ab Tak Chhappan 2, Mastram, and 1920 London. The actor has also played various characters on television shows such as Nisha Aur Uske Cousins, Powder, Seven, Hum Ne Li Hai…Shapath, Dharmakshetra, Anhoniyon Ka Andhera, Kuch Rang Pyar Ke Aise Bhi, Ganga Kii Dheej, CID, Gutur Gu 2, and Roop – Mard Ka Naya Swaroop.

Jagat Rawat Facts

  • In 2013, he appeared as MP Mahendra Pratap in the Hindi-language action film Commando: A One Man Army. It is the 1st installment of the Commando film series and stars Vidyut Jammwal, Pooja Chopra, and Jaideep Ahlawat.
  • Jagat Rawat played the recurring role of Mohan Nemichand Gangwal (Saroj and Nemichand’s son) in the Star Plus coming of age romantic drama series Nisha Aur Uske Cousins from 2014 to 2015.
  • He brought to life the character of Shuklaji in the 2015 Hindi-language biographical film Manjhi – The Mountain Man, starring Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Radhika Apte.
  • From 2018 to 2019, Jagat Rawat was cast as Rupesh Patel/Mr. Patel in the Colors TV drama series Roop – Mard Ka Naya Swaroop.

Aiyana Cristal- Professional Career

Right after her graduation Aiyana started working, never missing a chance to show her skills and aspiration to work. In 2005 Aiyana was lucky to initiate her career at Fox Sports, where she worked as a production assistant. A couple of years later Aiyana moved to Champaign, Illinois after she had an offer to work in news for WICD News Channel 15, where she obtained the position of a news reporter. She enjoyed working there for more than two years, changing her employer in 2010, before deciding that she was interested in sports reporting and managed to get a job at KTHV in August 2010, working as a sports anchor and a reporter.

She left this position in September 2012, switching to WPLG in the same position of a sports reporter for 13 months, then changing her employer again. Later she worked for Comcast Sportsnet Chicago, holding similar positions of reporter and anchor from September 2013 to the end of 2015. Since January 2016 Aiyana Cristal has been working as an anchor and reporter at WGCL/CBS46.

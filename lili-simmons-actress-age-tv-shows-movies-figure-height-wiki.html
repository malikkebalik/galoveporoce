Lili Simmons Wiki/Bio

Lili Simmons Age In 2023

Her birthday falls on July 23, 1993 in Cardiff-by-the-Sea, California, U.S. As of 2023, she is 30 years old. She grew up in Cardiff-by-the-Sea in San Diego County, California. Simmons was discovered by talent manager Kate Linden, at age 15.

She starred in the film Fat Kid Rules the World, in 2012. She was cast in what had been stated would be a recurring role in another series, Westworld, in 2016. She was cast in drama series Ray Donovan, in 2017. She was cast in Gotham in 2019.

Fans Say 'The Isley Brothers' Ron Isley Looks Youthful At 80 Next to Younger Wife Who Is Wearing a

Kandy Isley shared a picture of herself and her husband Ron Isley of “The Isley Brothers,” and fans took to the comments section to talk about how youthful Ron looked despite his age.

Aging is a concept everyone is familiar with, and although it varies for many people, there are some who have managed to retain their youthful looks despite their age.

One such person is Ron Isley of “The Isley Brothers.” This was seen when his wife of almost two decades, Kandy, shared a picture of them looking dolled up and in love.

Ron Isley & Kandy Johnson Isley at the Barnstable Brown Kentucky Derby Eve Gala on May 1, 2015 | Photo: Getty Images

The image showed Ron standing next to Kandy, who wore a tight body con dress with a low-cut neck. On the other hand, Ron looked delectable in his all-black suit which he paired with some sunshades.

Kandy opted to wear her curly blonde hair in a long shoulder-length style while Ron had a low-cut with his beard properly trimmed. The post had the caption:

“Tonight catch the Isley Brothers on Jimmy Kimmel Live @ 11:35 pm EST @theisleybrothers @ronaldisley #ISLEYBROTHERS.”

Following the post, fans and lovers of the couple took to the comments section to share their thoughts and opinions on how dapper they both looked. Someone expressed that it seemed Ron had found the fountain of youth as he looked incredibly great for someone his age.

The Isley Brothers would be making a comeback.

Fan's comments under a picture of Ronald Isley and his wife, Kandy, on Instagram | Photo: Instagram/kandyisley

Another person expressed excitement about the show that was to begin and complimented the couple saying they looked like a “million bucks” and were beautiful.

The post had thousands of likes and hundreds of comments from fans and followers of Kandy, with some saying that what Kandy and Ron had was beautiful to witness. Someone added:

“Whewwwww uncle Ron is looking younger and younger mercy you to [sic] look dammn good.”

Fan's comments under a picture of Ronald Isley and his wife, Kandy, on Instagram | Photo: Instagram/kandyisley

Born on May 21, 1941, in Cincinnati, Ohio, Ron is one half of a musical band he started over 50 years ago, and the band has gained notable acclaim in the music industry.

He was previously married to Margret Tinsley in 1960, and they welcomed a daughter named Tia in 1969. Following that, they got divorced, and Ron married Angela Winbush from 1993 to 2002.

His relationship with Kandy started when she, also a musician, signed under Ron’s management company. They dated for a year and got married in 2005. They have a son, Ronald Isley Junior, whom they welcomed in 2007.

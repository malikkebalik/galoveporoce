Blueface is well-known for both his success in music and his bare-knuckle boxing career. In June 2021, he made the announcement that he had signed up to participate in the Philadelphia-based Bare Knuckle Fighting Championship. He made his debut in July 2021 and defeated TikToker Kane Trujillo.

Blueface has a history of legal troubles, including repeated arrests. In November 2018, the rapper was apprehended and charged with shooting at an occupied car. He was released on a $69,000 bond, according to NME.

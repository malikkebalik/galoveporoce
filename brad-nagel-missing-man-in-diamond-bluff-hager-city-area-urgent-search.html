Brad Nagel Missing Man in Diamond Bluff/Hager City Area, Urgent Search

 Posted on July 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  392 words  |  Martina Birk

Law enforcement authorities in Red Wing, Minnesota, are actively seeking public assistance in the search for a missing man, Brad Nagel. Last heard from on Wednesday, November 15, 2023, at around 11:45 pm, Nagel’s disappearance has raised concerns among his friends, family, and the local community. A resident of Red Wing, Nagel was believed to be in the Diamond Bluff/Hager City area when he disappeared.

Brad Nagel Missing, What Actually Happened?

Brad Nagel was reported missing after his communication abruptly ceased on the night of November 15th. The details surrounding his disappearance remain unclear, causing increased anxiety among those who know him. He was last seen driving a 2005 Black Chevy Colorado with the license plate DLB 549. Given this information, the focus of the investigation is currently in the Diamond Bluff/Hager City area.

The Search Efforts

Local law enforcement, including the Goodhue County Sheriff’s Department, have been notified and are conducting an extensive search for Brad Nagel. They are gathering more information about his movements and contacts leading up to his last known communication. Simultaneously, they are also urging the public to be on the lookout for the black Chevy Colorado that Brad was last seen driving.

The Importance of Public Assistance

In such critical situations, community support can make a significant difference. Authorities are urging anyone with any information, no matter how insignificant it may seem, to come forward. Any piece of information could be crucial in helping locate Brad and ensuring his safety. If you have seen Brad Nagel or the vehicle he was driving, or if you have any information that could aid in the search, please contact the Goodhue County Sheriff’s Department at 651-385-3155 or your local law enforcement center.

The safety and well-being of Brad Nagel are of utmost importance. As the search continues, our thoughts are with his family and friends during this challenging time.
